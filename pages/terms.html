1. About NyumbaUG

NyumbaUG ("we", "us", "our") is an online marketplace platform that connects landlords and tenants for residential property rentals in Uganda. We are registered and operate under the laws of the Republic of Uganda.

NyumbaUG is a platform service only. We do not own, manage, or operate any of the properties listed on our platform. We are not a party to any rental agreement between landlords and tenants.

⚠️ NyumbaUG acts as an intermediary only. Any rental agreements, payments, and tenancy arrangements are strictly between the landlord and tenant. NyumbaUG bears no liability for disputes arising from such arrangements.

2. Eligibility & Registration

To use NyumbaUG, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a resident or citizen of Uganda, or have legal authority to enter into rental agreements in Uganda
  • Provide accurate, truthful, and complete information during registration
  • Maintain the security of your account password and not share it with others
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account

You may only create one account per person. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms.

3. Landlord Terms

As a landlord on NyumbaUG, you agree to:

  • Only list properties that you own or have legal authority to rent out
  • Provide accurate descriptions, prices, photos, and information about each property
  • Not list properties that are already rented, unavailable, or fictitious
  • Respond to tenant enquiries within a reasonable time
  • Update or remove listings when a property is rented or no longer available
  • Comply with all applicable Ugandan landlord-tenant laws and regulations
  • Pay the applicable subscription fee to access landlord features
  • Not solicit or accept payments from tenants through the NyumbaUG platform unless authorised

NyumbaUG reserves the right to review, approve, reject, or remove any listing at our sole discretion. All listings are subject to admin approval before going live.

4. Tenant Terms

As a tenant on NyumbaUG, you agree to:

  • Use the platform only for genuine property search purposes
  • Not misrepresent yourself or your intentions to landlords
  • Always view a property in person before making any payments
  • Never send money to a landlord before verifying the property is genuine
  • Report suspicious listings or fraudulent landlords to us immediately
  • Treat landlords and their properties with respect during viewings
🔒 Safety Notice: NyumbaUG will NEVER ask you to pay money through our platform to secure a property. Always meet in person, verify the property exists, and deal directly with the landlord. Be cautious of anyone requesting upfront payments before a viewing.

5. Payments & Subscriptions

NyumbaUG charges landlords a subscription fee to access listing and management features:

  • Monthly Plan: UGX 100,000 per month
  • Yearly Plan: UGX 480,000 per year (equivalent to UGX 40,000/month)

Payments are accepted via MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and bank transfer. Subscriptions are activated within 2 hours of payment verification.

Refund Policy: Subscription fees are non-refundable once activated. If you believe a payment error has occurred, contact us within 48 hours at hello@nyumbaug.co.ug.

We reserve the right to change subscription pricing with 30 days' notice to existing subscribers.

6. Listing Rules

All listings on NyumbaUG must:

  • Be for genuine residential properties located in Uganda
  • Include accurate photos of the actual property (not stock images)
  • State the correct monthly rent in Uganda Shillings (UGX)
  • Accurately describe the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, and amenities
  • Include a valid contact phone number for the landlord

The following are strictly prohibited in listings:

  • False, misleading, or exaggerated property descriptions
  • Photos taken from other listings or the internet
  • Properties listed at prices significantly different from the actual rent
  • Duplicate listings for the same property
  • Listings for properties outside Uganda
  • Commercial properties listed as residential
