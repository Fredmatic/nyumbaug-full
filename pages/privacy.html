We collect the following categories of personal information:

Category What We Collect How

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your NyumbaUG account
  • Display your listings to potential tenants
  • Facilitate communication between landlords and tenants
  • Process and verify subscription payments
  • Send you important account notifications and updates
  • Improve our platform based on how users interact with it
  • Detect and prevent fraud, scams, and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations under Ugandan law
  • Send you occasional marketing emails (you can unsubscribe at any time)
We will never sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Between users: Landlord contact details (name and phone) are shown to tenants on listing pages. Tenant details are shared with landlords only when an enquiry is submitted.
  • Service providers: We use Cloudinary (image hosting) and email service providers to operate our platform. These providers are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by Ugandan law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers: If NyumbaUG is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to our database is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We use JWT tokens for secure session management
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we take every reasonable precaution, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and never share your login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained for the life of your account plus 2 years after deletion
  • Listing data: Retained for 1 year after a listing is removed
  • Messages & enquiries: Retained for 2 years
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Ugandan tax law

After the applicable retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights

Under Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@nyumbaug.co.ug. We will respond within 21 days as required by Ugandan law.

8. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our platform:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences such as saved listings and filters.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how the platform is used (anonymised data only).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging in.

9. Children's Privacy

NyumbaUG is not intended for use by persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has registered, we will promptly delete their account and associated data.

If you believe a child has registered on our platform, please contact us at privacy@nyumbaug.co.ug.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an email notification to all registered users
  • Display a notice on the platform for 30 days

Continued use of NyumbaUG after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
