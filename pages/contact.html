Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to list a property? ▾
Yes! Listing your property on Nyumba UG is completely free. Premium features may be available in the future.
How do I contact a landlord? ▾
On any listing page you'll find the landlord's phone number and a direct WhatsApp link. Click to call or message them instantly.
Are listings verified? ▾
We review all listings before they go live. Always visit a property in person before making any payments.
How long does a listing take to go live? ▾
We review new listings within 24 hours. You'll be notified when your listing is approved.
What areas do you cover? ▾
We currently cover Kampala and Greater Kampala including Wakiso and Mukono. Expanding across Uganda soon.
